**Weaponsmiths** tend to the weapon shops in
[Bree](./Bree_Weaponsmith.md),
[Fornost](./Fornost.md#inhabitants) and
[Tharbad](./Tharbad.md#shops). They are
[level](./Level.md) ~19-20 mobiles.

years.
