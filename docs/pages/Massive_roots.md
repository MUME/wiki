**Massive roots** are enormous, ancient tree roots encountered deep within [Fangorn Forest](./Fangorn.md). They are among the hazards that make navigation through Fangorn difficult, forming natural barriers between rooms and contributing to the zone's sense of impenetrable, primordial forest.

In [MUME](./MUME.md), massive roots appear as environmental objects that may block movement or define room boundaries within the Fangorn zone. They are a reminder that Fangorn is not merely a forest but a living, dangerous presence — the domain of the [Ents](./Fangorn.md).
