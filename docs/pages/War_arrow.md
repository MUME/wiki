A **war arrow** is a heavy battle arrow used with bows in [MUME](./MUME.md). It is fired using the [Missile weapons](./Missile_weapons.md) skill.

> **consider arrow**
>
> Its accuracy is awful, and it inflicts little damage upon hitting. It weighs a few ounces.

Despite its poor accuracy rating, war arrows deal more damage than [ash arrows](./Ash_arrow.md) and are generally preferred by archers who want more stopping power. They are compatible with the [Elven shortbow](./Elven_shortbow.md) and [Orkish shortbow](./Orkish_shortbow.md). The accuracy penalty can be offset by a high [missile weapons](./Missile_weapons.md) skill level.
