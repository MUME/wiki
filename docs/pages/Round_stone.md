A **round stone** is ammunition used with the [sling](./Sling.md) in [MUME](./MUME.md). Stones are fired using the [Missile weapons](./Missile_weapons.md) skill.

> **consider stone**
>
> Its accuracy is awful, and it inflicts very little damage upon hitting. It weighs about an ounce.

Round stones are the weakest ranged ammunition in the game, with both the poorest accuracy and damage. However, they are extremely lightweight and essentially free to obtain, making a sling a viable backup ranged option for characters who do not wish to carry heavy arrows or bolts. Round stones are only compatible with the [sling](./Sling.md).
