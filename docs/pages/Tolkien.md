**J.R.R. Tolkien** (John Ronald Reuel Tolkien, 1892–1973) was an English writer, poet, philologist, and Oxford professor, best known as the author of *The Hobbit* (1937) and *The Lord of the Rings* (1954–55). His work created the fictional world of Arda, of which Middle-earth is a part — and forms the entire basis of [MUME](./MUME.md).

Tolkien's other major works include *The Silmarillion* (published posthumously), *Unfinished Tales*, and the twelve-volume *History of Middle-earth* series edited by his son Christopher. Many MUME locations, characters, and events draw on the wider legendarium beyond just *The Lord of the Rings*.
