A **metal-cased bolt** is a reinforced crossbow bolt with a solid metal casing, providing superior damage over standard bolts. It is fired from crossbows using the [Missile weapons](./Missile_weapons.md) skill.

> **consider bolt**
>
> Its accuracy is poor, and it inflicts average damage upon hitting. It weighs a few ounces.

Metal-cased bolts are the strongest standard bolt available, dealing average damage compared to the "little" or "very little" damage of [bolts](./Bolt.md) and [blackened bolts](./Blackened_bolt.md). They are compatible with both the [crossbow](./Crossbow.md) and the [light crossbow](./Light_crossbow.md). Their heavier weight means carrying capacity should be considered.
