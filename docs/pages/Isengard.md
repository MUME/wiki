**Isengard** is the fortress stronghold of [Saruman the White](./Saruman.md), situated at the southern end of the [Misty Mountains](./Misty_Mountains.md) where the mountains meet [Dunland](./Dunland.md) to the west and [Fangorn](./Fangorn.md) to the east. It is one of the most significant destinations in MUME for high-level players.

## In MUME

Isengard is a **New Hero destination**, generally suitable for players at [level](./Level.md) 21 and above.

- Saruman is a major NPC quest-giver who rewards players for completing tasks. Say **old** to speak with Saruman once inside.
- The guide **Yerik** can take players to Isengard.
- The fortress was assaulted and flooded by the [Ents](./Fangorn.md) of Fangorn, led by [Treebeard](./Treebeard.md), trapping Saruman within the central tower of Orthanc.
