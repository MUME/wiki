The **unarmed combat** skill is a [warrior](./Warrior.md)
[skill](./Skill.md), limited to [Trolls](./Troll.md) only,
that allows them to hit harder and better with their bare hands. Unarmed
Trolls need no [shield](./Shield.md) to [bash](./Bash.md)
successfully.
