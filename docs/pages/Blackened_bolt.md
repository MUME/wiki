A **blackened bolt** is a type of crossbow ammunition, darkened in appearance and lighter than a standard [bolt](./Bolt.md). It is fired from crossbows using the [Missile weapons](./Missile_weapons.md) skill.

> **consider bolt**
>
> Its accuracy is poor, and it inflicts very little damage upon hitting. It weighs about an ounce.

Being lighter than a standard bolt, blackened bolts are easier to carry in quantity. They deal less damage than [metal-cased bolts](./Metal-cased_bolt.md) and are compatible with the [light crossbow](./Light_crossbow.md) and [crossbow](./Crossbow.md). They may be favoured by dark-side players due to their appearance.
