Syntax : cast 'black breath'
Type : Offensive, special
Accumulative: N/A
Duration : No
Casting : Very long
Position : Standing
Level : Level 12
Class : Shaman
Min. Mana : 25
Example: \> cast 'black breath' dwarf

This [spell](./Spell.md) causes weakness and despair in others. It may be cast at a target and affects everyone nearby who is not in your party. The victims lose mana and the energy to move. Some of that mana flows to the caster who receives a boost in energy, granting greater mobility. The incantation is considered an evil act.

Deep insight and an iron will are quite important when learning this.

This spell can be learned from:

| Guild | Sessions |
|-------|--------|
| Brolg | 35 |
| East Priest | 25 |
