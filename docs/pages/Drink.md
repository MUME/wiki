**Syntax:** `drink [container]`

The **drink** command lets you consume a liquid, either from a [container](./Container.md) you are carrying or from a water source in the current [room](./room.md) (such as a stream, fountain, or well). Staying hydrated is important — thirst reduces your effectiveness in combat and regeneration.

If no container is specified, MUME will attempt to drink from a water source in the room. To drink from a container in your inventory, specify it by name.

## Liquid types

- **Water** — the most common liquid, found in fountains, streams, and water skins.
- **Alcohol** (ale, wine, spirits) — sold in inns and taverns; consumed in moderation.
- **Potions** — magical liquids with effects such as healing or curing blindness.
- **Herbal draughts** — brewed via [herblore](./Herb.md), can restore HP, mana, or movement.
