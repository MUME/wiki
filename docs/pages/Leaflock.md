**Leaflock** (Finglas in Elvish) is one of the three eldest [Ents](./Fangorn.md) of [Fangorn Forest](./Fangorn.md), alongside [Treebeard](./Treebeard.md) and Skinbark (Fladrif). He has become deeply drowsy and tree-like over the centuries, spending most of his time rooted in place and growing thick leaves in summer. He did not take part in the assault on [Isengard](./Isengard.md) — he had grown too much like a tree to march.

## In MUME

Leaflock appears as an ancient, largely stationary NPC within [Fangorn Forest](./Fangorn.md). He is one of the powerful Huorn-like entities that make Fangorn dangerous to traverse for dark-side players.
