**Leaflock** (Finglas in Elvish) is one of the three eldest [Ents](./Fangorn.md) of [Fangorn Forest](./Fangorn.md). He is described as having become very sleepy and tree-like over the centuries, spending most of his time standing still and growing leaves in summer. He did not take part in the assault on [Isengard](./Isengard.md).
