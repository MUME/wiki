**Belts** occupy the waist slot in [MUME](./MUME.md). Unlike most armour, belts typically provide no physical absorption — their value lies in magical bonuses such as dodge bonus (DB), move regeneration, or saving spell bonuses.

Notable belts include:

- **Gleaming belt** — DB +2, move regen +3 (Magic)
- **Broad silver belt** — DB +3 (Magic)
- **Golden belt** — move regen +3, Saving Spell -10 (Magic)
- **Belt of fell hide** — move and HP regen +3 (Magic)

For full stats of all belts, see the identified gear list in [Armour](./Armour.md#belts).

Belts are primarily sought for their move regeneration or dodge bonuses. The golden belt and gleaming belt are popular choices for players who want both mobility and defensive utility.
