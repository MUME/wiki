A **bolt** is the standard ammunition for [crossbows](./Crossbow.md) in [MUME](./MUME.md). Bolts are fired using the [Missile weapons](./Missile_weapons.md) skill.

> **consider bolt**
>
> Its accuracy is poor, and it inflicts little damage upon hitting. It weighs a few ounces.

Bolts are compatible with both the [crossbow](./Crossbow.md) and the [light crossbow](./Light_crossbow.md). They are widely available from shops. For better performance, consider [metal-cased bolts](./Metal-cased_bolt.md), which deal average damage, or [blackened bolts](./Blackened_bolt.md).
