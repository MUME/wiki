The **Orkish shortbow** is a shortbow crafted by Orcs in [MUME](./MUME.md). It is used with the [Missile weapons](./Missile_weapons.md) skill and fires [arrows](./Ash_arrow.md) as ammunition.

> **consider bow**
>
> Its accuracy is poor, its speed very fast, and it causes average damage. It weighs three pounds.

The Orkish shortbow is the dark-side counterpart to the [Elven shortbow](./Elven_shortbow.md), with identical stats: very fast fire rate at the cost of poor accuracy. At only three pounds it is one of the lightest ranged weapons available. Compatible ammunition includes [ash arrows](./Ash_arrow.md) and [war arrows](./War_arrow.md).
