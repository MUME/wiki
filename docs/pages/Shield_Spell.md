**Shield** is a level 13 [mage](./Mage.md) [spell](./Spell.md) that increases the [dodge bonus](./Dodge_bonus.md) of the caster. The effectiveness of the shield is based on the caster's [dexterity](./Statistic.md#dexterity) and [dodge](./Dodge.md) skill. Generally considered a must-have spell for all casters.

This spell can be learned from:

| Guild | Sessions |
|-------|--------|
| [Forlond Mage Guild](./Forlond_Mage_Guild.md) | 6 |
| [Harlond Mage Guild](./Harlond_Mage_Guild.md) | 3 |
| [Lórien Mage Guild](./Lórien_Mage_Guild.md) | 16 |
| [Rivendell Mage Guild](./Rivendell_Mage_Guild.md) | 12 |
| [Tharbad Mage Guild](./Tharbad_Mage_Guild.md) | 10 |
