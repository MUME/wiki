The **Ancient Broken Road** is a crumbling, ancient path that runs southward from the moorlands near [Rivendell](./Rivendell.md) down to the ruins of [Eregion](./Eregion.md). The road was once a major thoroughfare between the Elven realms of Rivendell and Eregion during the Second Age, but it has long since fallen into disrepair and is now a difficult route through wilderness.

The road passes through varied terrain and is used by players as a path between [Rivendell](./Rivendell.md) and [Tharbad](./Tharbad.md) via [Eregion](./Eregion.md). Travellers should be prepared for hazards along the way.
