MUME offers playable races on both sides of the conflict. **Free Peoples** characters start in Bree or other white-side towns, while **Shadow** characters — [Orcs](./Orc.md), [Trolls](./Troll.md), and [Black Númenóreans](./Black_Númenórean.md) — begin in dark-side territory. Each race has distinct stat bonuses, racial abilities, and roleplaying traditions.
