The **Brush** is a stretch of wild, brushy terrain lying between Weathertop (east of [Bree](./Bree.md)) and the Windswepts that lead up toward [Rivendell](./Rivendell.md). It forms part of the main east–west road connecting Bree to Rivendell.

## In MUME

The Brush is a transitional zone that adventurers pass through on the journey from Bree toward Rivendell. It is characterised by:

- Thick underbrush and wild terrain that makes travel more costly.
- Wildlife and hostile mobs suited to mid-level players.
- Proximity to Weathertop, which lies to the west.

The Brush is not a destination in itself but an important waypoint on one of MUME's main travel routes.
