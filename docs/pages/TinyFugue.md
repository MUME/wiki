**TinyFugue** (commonly called **tf**) is a long-running console-based MUD client with an extensive macro and trigger system. It has been used by [MUME](./MUME.md) players for decades and has a dedicated community of veteran players who maintain tf script collections for MUME.

TinyFugue supports multiple simultaneous connections, a rich built-in scripting language, and many protocol features. While it is less actively developed than [Tintin++](./Tintin++.md), its user base remains loyal to its unique feature set.
