**Syntax:** `mix <herb> [in <kit>]`

The **mix** command combines herbs in a [herbal kit](./Herbal_kit.md) to create herbal draughts. This is the core action of the [Herblore](./Herb.md) system, which allows characters to brew potions that restore hit points, movement, or cure various conditions.

To mix herbs, you need:

1. A [herbal kit](./Herbal_kit.md) — the container used for brewing.
2. The appropriate herbs — gathered from the wild or purchased.
3. Knowledge of which herbs combine for which effect (see [Herb](./Herb.md)).

Before mixing, you can [crush](./Crush.md) herbs to prepare them. The resulting draught can be [drunk](./Drink.md) for its effect.
