**Grocers** tend to groceries and general stores in
[Bree](Bree "wikilink"), [Fornost](Fornost "wikilink") and
[Tharbad](Tharbad "wikilink"). They are [level](level "wikilink") ~16-17
[mobiles](mobile "wikilink").
