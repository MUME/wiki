\> **look gruel**

This is a bowl of cracked corn, barley, and other local grains, boiled
up into

a thick porridge. Perhaps not the tastiest of meals but cheap and
filling.

\> **consider gruel**

It is a bowl of gruel.

It weighs about one pound.
